About Us

Ectotherm ER is a curriculum for middle school students used to foster awareness of the scientific method, climate change, and citizen science. Students collect temperature data from agar frogs placed in a variety of environments and conduct their own analyses. The data is then submitted to the Richards-Zawacki lab, which utilizes it for their research. As the program expands beyond classrooms in Western Pennsylvania, a centralized repository for instructions, materials, and data submission has become necessary. Therefore, we are developing a website for the Ectotherm ER project that will hold materials for teachers and students, allow teachers to submit data produced by their classes, and display that data via a graph plugin.
